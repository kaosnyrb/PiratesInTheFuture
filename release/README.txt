User Guide
To install extract the contents of the archive into a folder and run the executable "Pirates in the future.exe".
The proof of concept shows the basic combat system and the water effect, to control your ship use the arrow keys or WASD.
To fire your cannon press space or left click the mouse.
The water effects are shown when you shoot an enemy ship.
